## SECTION A: Business Fundamentals

### A1: Legal Business Name
- **Status**: `REQUIRED`
- **Answer Format**: Single-line text
- **Example**: "Acme Home Services LLC" or "Acme Home Services (DBA: Acme Plumbing)"
- **Note**: Include DBA (Doing Business As) name if different from legal name

---

### A2: Industry Type
- **Status**: `REQUIRED`
- **Answer Format**: Single choice (select one)
- **Options**:
  - `Professional Services` (Consulting, Legal, Accounting, Marketing)
  - `Home Services` (Plumbing, HVAC, Electrical, Landscaping, Cleaning)
  - `Healthcare & Wellness` (Medical, Dental, Therapy, Fitness)
  - `Retail & E-commerce` (Online store, Physical shop, Both)
  - `Education & Training` (Tutoring, Courses, Workshops)
  - `Real Estate & Property` (Buying, Selling, Property Management)
  - `Hospitality & Food` (Restaurant, Hotel, Catering)
  - `Non-Profit & Community`
  - `Technology / SaaS`
  - `Other`: `___________` (specify)

---

### A3: Physical Locations & Service Area
- **Status**: `REQUIRED`
- **Answer Format**: Text list + geographic scope
- **Fields**:
  1. **Primary Location**: (City, State)
  2. **Additional Locations**: (List if multiple offices)
  3. **Service Area**: (Circle one: `City only` | `County-wide` | `Multi-state` | `National` | `International`)
  4. **Specific Areas Served**: (List cities, neighborhoods, or regions)

- **Example**:
  - Primary Location: Austin, Texas
  - Additional Locations: San Marcos, Texas
  - Service Area: County-wide
  - Specific Areas Served: Travis County, Hays County, Williamson County

---

### A4: Years in Operation
- **Status**: `REQUIRED`
- **Answer Format**: Numeric
- **Options**:
  - `< 1 year` (Startup)
  - `1-3 years`
  - `4-10 years`
  - `11-20 years`
  - `20+ years`

---

### A5: Business Description (Elevator Pitch)
- **Status**: `REQUIRED`
- **Answer Format**: 2-3 sentences (50-150 words)
- **Prompt**: "Describe what your business does, who you serve, and what makes you different from competitors."

- **Anti-Ambiguity Example**:
  - **Vague**: "We help people with their homes."
  - **Clear**: "We provide eco-friendly home cleaning services for busy families in Austin, using only plant-based products and offering same-day appointments."

---

### A6: Services/Products Offered
- **Status**: `REQUIRED`
- **Answer Format**: List (3-8 items recommended)
- **Format**: For each service, provide:
  - Service Name
  - 1-sentence description focused on customer benefit

- **Example**:
  1. **Residential Plumbing** - We handle all home plumbing repairs and installations, from leaky faucets to full bathroom remodels.
  2. **Emergency Services** - 24/7 emergency plumbing response for burst pipes, clogged drains, and water heater failures.
  3. **Water Heater Installation** - Expert installation and maintenance of tankless and traditional water heaters.

---

### A7: Top 3 Business Goals for 2026
- **Status**: `REQUIRED`
- **Answer Format**: Multi-select (choose at least 2)
- **Options** (check all that apply):
  - [ ] Increase online leads and inquiries
  - [ ] Increase direct online sales
  - [ ] Improve brand awareness and visibility
  - [ ] Establish stronger local market presence
  - [ ] Expand to new geographic markets
  - [ ] Launch new product or service line
  - [ ] Improve customer support and communication
  - [ ] Reduce manual workload through automation
  - [ ] Other: `___________`

## SECTION E: Operational Pain Points & Process

### E1: Operational Pain Points
- **Status**: `OPTIONAL` (highly recommended for automation opportunities)
- **Answer Format**: List with scoring (1-5 scale)

**Prompt**: "List any operational challenges or repetitive tasks in your business. Rate each on time investment and frequency."

- **Format**:
  - **Pain Point**: (Describe the task/problem in 1 sentence)
  - **Time Investment** (1-5 scale):
    - `1` = Minutes per week
    - `3` = Hours per week
    - `5` = Hours per day
  - **Frequency** (1-5 scale):
    - `1` = Rarely (monthly or less)
    - `3` = Weekly
    - `5` = Multiple times daily

- **Example**:
  - **Pain Point**: Answering the same customer questions repeatedly via phone/email
  - **Time Investment**: `4` (several hours per week)
  - **Frequency**: `5` (daily)

- **List your pain points** (up to 10):
  1. Pain Point: `_____________________`
     - Time Investment: `___`
     - Frequency: `___`

  2. Pain Point: `_____________________`
     - Time Investment: `___`
     - Frequency: `___`

  3. Pain Point: `_____________________`
     - Time Investment: `___`
     - Frequency: `___`

  4. Pain Point: `_____________________`
     - Time Investment: `___`
     - Frequency: `___`

  5. Pain Point: `_____________________`
     - Time Investment: `___`
     - Frequency: `___`
